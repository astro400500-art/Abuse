<h3>Introduction</h3>
<p>
Abuse Is A Serious Issue That Affects Millions Of People Worldwide.
</p>
<p>
It Can Happen In Homes, Schools, Workplaces, And Online Spaces.
</p>
<p>
Abuse Is Never The Victim’s Fault.
</p>

<h3>What Is Abuse?</h3>
<p>
Abuse Refers To Harmful Behaviors Used To Control Or Hurt Another Person.
</p>
<p>
These Behaviors May Be Intentional Or Repeated Over Time.
</p>
<p>Conceptual Representation:</p>
<img src="https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5f07e64b122ca1020e14d5fe/1594609155469-HAPXP98RMI0TDAEYZFSH/05+rabbit+m+copy.jpg" width="300">
<p><em>Visual Explanation Of Abuse.</em></p>
<hr>
<h3>Types Of Abuse</h3>
<p>There Are Several Recognized Forms Of Abuse:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Physical Abuse – Causing Bodily Harm.</li>
    <li>Emotional Abuse – Damaging Self-Worth.</li>
    <li>Verbal Abuse – Using Harmful Language.</li>
    <li>Sexual Abuse – Any Non-Consensual Sexual Act.</li>
    <li>Neglect – Failure To Provide Basic Care.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Physical Abuse</h3>
<p>
Physical Abuse Involves Direct Bodily Harm.
</p>
<p>
This May Include Hitting, Pushing, Or Restraining Someone.
</p>
<img src="https://www.psychiatryadvisor.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/8/2018/12/childabuseg157502084_1487023.jpg" width="300">
<p><em>Physical Abuse Awareness Illustration.</em></p>
<hr>
<h3>Emotional And Verbal Abuse</h3>
<p>
Emotional Abuse Can Be Harder To Notice.
</p>
<p>
It Includes Insults, Threats, Manipulation, And Humiliation.
</p>
<img src="https://i.pinimg.com/736x/b7/65/09/b76509172b165c2c304b4cdb4f9ea8db.jpg" width="300">
<p><em>Emotional Abuse Effects.</em></p>
<hr>
<h3>Signs Of Abuse</h3>
<p>Common Warning Signs May Include:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Sudden Changes In Behavior</li>
    <li>Fear Around Specific Individuals</li>
    <li>Unexplained Injuries</li>
    <li>Withdrawal From Friends Or Family</li>
</ul>
<p>Warning Signs Illustration:</p>
<img src="https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1080/1*cdoLJQHq-s6oqkGRKFFC1g.jpeg" width="300">

<hr>
<h3>Effects Of Abuse</h3>
<p>
Abuse Can Cause Long-Term Emotional And Physical Damage.
</p>
<p>
Survivors May Experience Anxiety, Depression, Or Trauma.
</p>
<img src="https://i0.wp.com/hopefulpanda.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/Effects-of-Childhood-Abuse.png?resize=512%2C768&ssl=1" width="300">
<p><em>Long-Term Effects Of Abuse.</em></p>

<h3>How To Help Someone</h3>
<p>
Supporting A Victim Requires Patience And Understanding.
</p>
<ul>
    <li>Listen Without Judgment</li>
    <li>Offer Support</li>
    <li>Encourage Professional Help</li>
</ul>
